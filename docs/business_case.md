# NBA Revenue Intelligence — Business Case

## Executive Summary

Professional sports organizations generate significant revenue through ticket sales, premium seating, marketing campaigns, and fan engagement. Maximizing revenue requires accurately forecasting ticket demand, optimizing pricing strategies, monitoring inventory, understanding customer purchasing behavior, and measuring marketing effectiveness.

NBA Revenue Intelligence is an end-to-end analytics platform that simulates the responsibilities of a revenue analytics team supporting a professional basketball organization. The platform combines data engineering, dimensional modeling, machine learning, and business intelligence to transform operational data into actionable insights that support executive decision-making.

---

## Business Problem

Ticketing and business operations teams must make pricing and marketing decisions before every game throughout the season.

Typical business questions include:

- Which games are likely to sell out?
- Which games require promotional campaigns?
- How should ticket prices change based on expected demand?
- Which customer segments generate the most revenue?
- Which marketing channels produce the highest conversion rates?
- Where are customers abandoning the online purchase process?
- What revenue should leadership expect for upcoming games?

Without a centralized analytics platform, answering these questions often requires combining multiple reports, manual analysis, and historical assumptions rather than predictive insights.

---

## Proposed Solution

NBA Revenue Intelligence provides a modern analytics workflow that combines publicly available NBA schedule information with a realistic enterprise ticketing simulation to model customer behavior, pricing strategies, promotions, inventory, and web conversion funnels.

The platform automates the complete analytics lifecycle by:

- Simulating enterprise-scale ticketing and customer activity
- Building a dimensional SQL data warehouse
- Engineering analytical features for machine learning
- Segmenting games using K-Means clustering
- Forecasting ticket demand and revenue using regression models
- Generating executive KPIs and business recommendations
- Producing dashboard-ready datasets for Power BI

---

## Business Capabilities

The platform enables stakeholders to:

- Monitor ticket revenue and sales performance
- Track inventory utilization and sell-through rates
- Evaluate pricing strategies
- Measure marketing campaign effectiveness
- Analyze customer purchasing behavior
- Monitor website conversion funnels
- Forecast ticket demand and revenue
- Identify inventory risk and promotional opportunities
- Support executive decision-making through interactive dashboards

---

## Expected Business Value

The platform demonstrates how modern analytics can improve business performance by enabling leadership to:

- Increase ticket revenue through data-driven pricing decisions
- Improve marketing return on investment through targeted promotions
- Reduce inventory risk for lower-demand games
- Improve customer conversion by identifying checkout friction
- Produce more accurate revenue forecasts
- Deliver executive-ready reporting through interactive dashboards
